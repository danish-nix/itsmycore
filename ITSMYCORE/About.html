The Company

Incorporated in 2017, ITSMYCORE is a continuation of more than a decade of experience and expertise in delivering technical, engineering, project management, consultancy and network infrastructure solutions and services to private and public sectors and entities. It is part of ITSMYGROUP’s broad portfolios and representation.   ITSMYCORE provides total, comprehensive and integrated business solution to assist our clients to achieve better efficiency, efficiency and optimization. ITSMYCORE is 100% Bumiputera, young, professional and energetic team who is wiling to provide and deliver our continuous support at high standards and adheres to client’s core values and requirement across flexible and various kind of project management, consultancy, contracting, trading, business and technical services We started with only 10 people, the company has experienced a significant business growth for over 5 years. We have expanded our core services and teams as well as engaged with more partners. We have now approximately 100 headcounts and technical supports worldwide.


Mission

We are dedicated in assisting our clients to provide various kind of services, trading and expertice; technical supports, workforce, consulting services and assistance for their business and technical difficulties and workplan by integrating the core elements and issues in line with industry's best practice.





Vision

To be the proven and chosen total solution provider for our corporate, private and public clients and community in various industries; IT related, systems and networks, engineering, telecommunication, construction, consulting and project management.
